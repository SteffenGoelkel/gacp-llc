Privacy Policy

Effective March 2026. How we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register for an account, we collect your name, email address, date of birth, phone number, company name, business registration details (EIN, corporate registration number, VAT), physical address, website URL, account type, business category, and intended use of products.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect your IP address, approximate geographic location (city, region, country) derived from your IP address, ISP/organization information, browser type and version, and device information.

Location Data

We use Cloudflare's built-in geolocation services to determine your approximate location. This is used for product availability filtering based on jurisdictional restrictions, fraud prevention during the registration process, and location verification against your stated address. We do not use GPS or precise location tracking.

Contact Form Submissions

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email, company (optional), subject, message content, and your IP address and approximate location at the time of submission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process and manage your account application; verify your identity, age, and business legitimacy during the approval process; provide access to our product catalogue and documentation appropriate to your account tier; communicate with you about your account, orders, and application status; restrict product visibility based on jurisdictional regulations; prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse; comply with legal and regulatory requirements; and improve our website and services.

3. Corporate Verification

As part of our account approval process, we may verify business applicant information against publicly available corporate registries (such as OpenCorporates, state secretary of state databases, or other public records). This verification is conducted to confirm the existence and active status of business entities applying for trade accounts.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by the following third-party service providers:

Supabase — database and authentication, hosted in the United States (East US region). Cloudflare — website hosting (Pages), DNS management, edge security, and Workers for email processing. MailChannels — transactional email delivery for contact form submissions and account notifications.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL) for all connections, row-level security (RLS) on all database records, role-based access controls restricting data access by account type, secure password hashing via Supabase Auth, and regular security review of access policies.

5. Payment Processing

Payment processing is performed by third-party payment providers. GACP LLC does not store, process, or have access to credit card numbers, bank account details, or other payment instrument data. All payment data is handled directly by our payment processor in compliance with PCI-DSS standards.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with service providers who assist in operating our website and business (as listed in Section 4), payment processors for order fulfillment, legal authorities when required by law, subpoena, or court order, and professional advisors (legal, accounting) under confidentiality obligations. All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and may only use it for the specific purposes for which it was shared.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses the following browser storage mechanisms:

Session storage (browser-based, cleared when the tab closes) — to maintain your login state, cache your profile data for performance, and store your detected geographic location during the browsing session. Local storage — to persist your shopping cart between sessions.

We do not currently use third-party analytics cookies, advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking technologies. If we introduce analytics or tracking tools in the future, this policy will be updated and, where required, consent will be obtained.

8. Your Rights

All Users

Regardless of your jurisdiction, you may request access to the personal information we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate information, request deletion of your account and associated data, and withdraw consent for data processing where consent was the legal basis.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and the California Privacy Rights Act, including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion of your personal information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right of access (Article 15), the right to rectification (Article 16), the right to erasure (Article 17), the right to restriction of processing (Article 18), the right to data portability (Article 20), and the right to object to processing (Article 21). Our legal basis for processing your data is contract performance (account management and order fulfillment), legitimate interest (fraud prevention, security, product restriction enforcement), and consent (where specifically obtained). To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us through our contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data according to the following schedule:

Active account data — retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Closed account data — deleted within 30 days of account closure, subject to legal retention requirements. Contact form submissions — retained for 24 months for business record purposes. IP and geolocation data — retained for 12 months for fraud prevention and compliance. Order and transaction records — retained for 7 years as required by US tax law.

If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Certain data may be retained longer where required by law or for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims.

10. Children's and Minors' Privacy

Our website and services are restricted to individuals aged 21 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 21. If you believe someone under 21 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

11. International Data Transfers

GACP LLC is based in the United States. If you access our website from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our website, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States. For EEA/UK users, such transfers are conducted under appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR, including standard contractual clauses where applicable.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law, including within 72 hours where required by GDPR.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Where changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you (such as via email or a notice on the website).

14. Contact and Data Protection Inquiries

For questions about this Privacy Policy, to exercise your data rights, or to file a privacy complaint, please contact us through our contact page. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.
