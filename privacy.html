Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GACP LLC ("GACP," "we," "us," or "our") operates the website gacp.llc. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you apply for a trade account, we collect: email address, company name, and intended use. If you complete your business profile, we may also collect: your name, phone number, business address, EIN/Tax ID, and company registration details.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location (via ipinfo.io) to determine service availability
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics (GA4)

Cookies and Tracking

We use the following cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — for website usage analytics
  • Session storage — for geo-location caching (expires after 10 minutes)
  • Supabase authentication tokens — to maintain your login session

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process trade account applications and verify business credentials
  • Provide access to our wholesale catalogue, pricing, and documentation
  • Fulfill orders, sample requests, and formulation enquiries
  • Send transactional emails related to your account or orders
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our website (Cloudflare for hosting, Supabase for authentication, Google for analytics)
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business partners only with your explicit consent

Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your trade account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of analytics tracking

California Residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@gacp.llc.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication via Supabase, and access controls on our database.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business use and are not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
