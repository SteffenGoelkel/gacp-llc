Quality & Testing

Third-party tested. Fully documented. Every ingredient, every lot.

Our Commitment

Every product in the GACP catalogue is backed by documentation you can trust. We work exclusively with cGMP-certified manufacturers and require third-party laboratory verification for every ingredient we supply.

Third-Party Testing

We do not rely solely on supplier self-testing. Our quality assurance process includes:

Identity & Purity

Each product is verified for botanical identity, standardized compound content, and absence of adulterants. Testing methods include HPLC, UV spectrophotometry, and gravimetric analysis as appropriate to the ingredient.

Microbiological Screening

All products are tested for total aerobic microbial count, E. coli, Salmonella, Listeria, yeast, and mold to ensure they meet or exceed USP and AOAC standards.

Heavy Metals

Products are screened for arsenic, cadmium, lead, and mercury, with limits set at or below 1.0 ppm per element.

Mycotoxin Screening

Where applicable, products are tested for aflatoxins (B1, B2, G1, G2) and ochratoxin A (OTA).

Certificates of Analysis

Every product in our catalogue has a corresponding Certificate of Analysis (CoA) available to approved trade account holders. Our CoAs include:

  • Product identification and GACP product code
  • Botanical name and country of origin
  • Physical characteristics (form, color, odor, moisture, particle size)
  • Full microbiological panel with test methods
  • Heavy metals analysis
  • Certification status (organic, kosher, allergen-free)
  • Storage conditions and shelf life

CoAs are available for download through the trade portal. Sample CoAs can be provided upon request for prospective buyers.

Certifications We Work With

Our suppliers hold certifications including:

  • USDA Organic / EcoCert Organic
  • Kosher Pareve
  • SQF Level 2
  • cGMP Compliant
  • Non-GMO
  • Gluten-Free
  • BSE/TSE-Free

Supply Chain Transparency

We maintain full traceability from raw material origin through processing and delivery. Every product listing includes country of origin for raw materials and the location of processing facilities. We work directly with established manufacturers — not brokers — to ensure consistency and accountability.

Allergen Controls

Our suppliers operate dedicated facilities with comprehensive allergen management programs. Each product comes with a detailed allergen statement confirming the absence of major allergens in the product, on shared production lines, and within the manufacturing facility.
