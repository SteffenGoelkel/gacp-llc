Restricted Products & Account Verification

Tiered access, verification, and jurisdictional controls.

Overview

GACP LLC operates a controlled-access wholesale platform. Not all products are available to all accounts, and not all accounts are approved for purchase. This policy explains GACP's approach to product access, account verification, and jurisdictional controls.

1. Account Tiers

1.1 Public Catalogue. GACP's public product catalogue is viewable without an account. It displays product identity, source, physical characteristics, and pricing for standard inventory items. Transactional features (ordering, CoA access, formulation requests) require a registered and approved account.

1.2 Standard Trade Account. Registered buyers who have completed GACP's verification process and been approved may access standard inventory, place orders, download Certificates of Analysis, and submit formulation requests.

1.3 Enhanced Verification Account. Certain products designated as "Restricted" require enhanced verification before access is granted. Enhanced verification may include:

  • Confirmation of active business registration in the Buyer's jurisdiction;
  • A signed intended-use declaration specifying the Buyer's application for the restricted product;
  • Documentation of relevant licenses, permits, or regulatory approvals held by the Buyer; and
  • Confirmation that the Buyer's jurisdiction permits the purchase, possession, and use of the specific product.

2. Restricted Product Criteria

A product may be designated as Restricted based on one or more of the following factors:

  • The product is subject to state-level bans or restrictions in one or more U.S. jurisdictions;
  • The product's regulatory classification is evolving or subject to ongoing agency review;
  • The product contains compounds that require additional buyer diligence regarding downstream labeling, safety, or compliance;
  • Payment processor or banking partner requirements necessitate enhanced controls for the product category; or
  • GACP determines, at its sole discretion, that additional verification is appropriate for the product.

3. Jurisdictional Controls

Product availability is controlled dynamically by jurisdiction. GACP does not publish a static list of restricted jurisdictions. Instead, product availability may be controlled through a combination of:

  • Database-level product visibility flags tied to jurisdictional rules;
  • Account-level address and IP geolocation screening; and
  • Manual review of orders for restricted products prior to shipment.

If a product is not available in the Buyer's jurisdiction, it will not appear in the Buyer's catalogue view or will be flagged as unavailable at checkout.

4. GACP's Discretion

GACP reserves the right to refuse any order, restrict access to any product, require additional verification at any time, and terminate any account that GACP believes, in its sole discretion, may be associated with non-compliant, fraudulent, or unlawful activity.
