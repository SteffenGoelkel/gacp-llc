How to Order

GACP serves manufacturers, formulators, and brand owners purchasing botanical extracts and bioceutical ingredients in commercial quantities. Our ordering process follows standard B2B wholesale conventions — quote-driven, invoice-based, with full documentation at every step.

The process

1. Request a quote

Add the products you need to your Quote Builder in the trade portal, then submit. If you're working on a custom blend or contract manufacturing project, use the Formulation Request form instead.

Either way, we respond within one US business day with a detailed pro forma invoice covering specifications, batch availability, your tier pricing, lead time, and freight terms.

2. Review and confirm

Confirm the pro forma by reply email, or by countersigning and returning a signed copy if your procurement process requires it. We hold quoted pricing for 14 days from issue.

3. Pay and we ship

Settle the pro forma via wire transfer or ACH. Bank details are on every invoice. We ship within two US business days of cleared payment, with tracking and the full documentation pack delivered at dispatch.

Payment terms

First orders. Wire transfer or ACH against pro forma invoice. We don't accept credit card payments.

Repeat accounts. Net-30 terms available to qualifying buyers after three completed orders, or following a successful credit application. Net-60 considered case-by-case for high-volume accounts.

International orders. Wire transfer or letter of credit. We ship globally except where botanical import restrictions apply — please reach out before placing your first international order so we can confirm clearance for your jurisdiction.

Lead times

Order type Typical lead time
In-stock items Ships within 2 business days of cleared payment
Bulk quantities (25 kg+) 5–10 business days, depending on lot availability
Custom blends and formulation work 2–4 weeks from formula sign-off

International freight adds 3–10 business days depending on destination and shipping mode.

Samples

Samples are available at $50 per sample. Postage is quoted separately and can be arranged on your shipping account (FedEx, DHL, or UPS) or covered by GACP and added to your invoice.

Sample requests are submitted directly from each product page in the catalogue. We email a $50 invoice with bank transfer details; samples ship once payment clears.

Larger evaluation quantities are quoted at cost.

Documentation

Every shipment includes:

  • Certificate of Analysis (lab-issued, batch-specific)
  • Certificate of Origin
  • Material Safety Data Sheet
  • Heavy metals, microbial, and pesticide testing data
  • Country-specific compliance documents on request

CoAs for current batches are accessible to trade-account holders via the CoA Library in the portal.
