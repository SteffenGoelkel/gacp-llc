Our Story

About GACP LLC

Bridging traditional botanical knowledge with modern analytical science.

Sourced at Origin, Refined in the Lab

GACP LLC was founded on a simple conviction: the botanical supply chain can be better. Too many ingredients pass through too many hands, losing traceability and quality at every step.

We work directly with GACP-compliant farms across Southeast Asia, Southern Africa, and the traditional cultivation regions of China and India. Our extracts and isolates are produced in audited facilities, standardised by HPLC, and accompanied by third-party Certificates of Analysis.

The result is a catalogue of botanical ingredients where every gram can be traced from field to formulation — and where purity is verified, not assumed.

Traceability

Full chain-of-custody documentation from source farm to finished product. GPS-tagged harvests, phytosanitary certificates, and batch-level CoA.

Precision

HPLC-standardised extracts and isolates with verified potency. Every batch tested against specification before release.

Dual-Layer Intelligence

Our unique consumer/trade data architecture protects formulation IP while providing retail customers with branded, compliant product information.

"Every gram traceable. Every batch verified. No exceptions."
