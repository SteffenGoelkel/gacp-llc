Policies

Shipping & Returns

Effective March 2026. Our shipping, returns, and refund policies.

Domestic Shipping (United States)

GACP LLC ships to all US states where the ordered products are legally permitted. Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days. Standard shipping is via USPS, UPS, or FedEx depending on package weight and destination. Estimated transit times are 3–7 business days for standard shipping, 2–3 business days for expedited shipping, and 1–2 business days for overnight/express shipping. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight, dimensions, and destination. Free shipping may be available for orders above certain thresholds — check your account tier for details.

Signature Requirement

Orders containing age-restricted products or orders exceeding $500 in value require signature confirmation upon delivery. GACP LLC is not responsible for packages marked as delivered by the carrier where signature was not required. For high-value orders, we strongly recommend selecting a shipping option that includes signature confirmation and insurance.

International Shipping

GACP LLC may ship internationally on a case-by-case basis. International orders are subject to additional review for regulatory compliance. The buyer is solely responsible for all import duties, taxes, customs fees, and regulatory clearances in the destination country. GACP LLC is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing. Contact us before placing an international order to confirm availability and shipping options.

Customs Liability

Customers are solely responsible for ensuring that products can be legally imported into their jurisdiction. GACP LLC is not responsible for packages seized, held, or destroyed by customs authorities. GACP LLC will not provide refunds or replacements for shipments confiscated by customs due to regulatory non-compliance in the destination country. It is the buyer's responsibility to research and comply with all applicable import laws, including controlled substance regulations, phytosanitary requirements, and any required import permits or licenses.

Restricted Product Shipping

Products classified as restricted are subject to additional shipping considerations. GACP LLC will not ship restricted products to jurisdictions where they are prohibited. Orders placed to restricted jurisdictions will be cancelled and refunded. It is the buyer's responsibility to confirm the legal status of products in their shipping destination.

Kratom (Mitragyna speciosa) Products

GACP LLC does not ship kratom products to the following US states: Alabama, Arkansas, Indiana, Rhode Island, Vermont, and Wisconsin. International restrictions apply to: Australia, Denmark, Finland, Israel, Lithuania, Malaysia, Myanmar, New Zealand, Poland, Romania, Russia, Sweden, Thailand, Vietnam, United Kingdom, Ireland, and Italy. This list may not be exhaustive — additional city and county restrictions may apply. Verify local regulations before ordering.

Cannabinoid (Hemp-derived) Products

All hemp-derived products sold by GACP LLC contain less than 0.3% delta-9 THC and are compliant with the US Farm Bill. However, state-level regulations may vary. International restrictions apply to: Russia, China, Japan, South Korea, Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, Turkey, Ukraine, and Belarus.

Kanna (Sceletium tortuosum) Products

GACP LLC does not ship kanna products to Australia or to Louisiana (US).

GACP LLC reserves the right to refuse or cancel orders for restricted products at its sole discretion. All restricted product shipments are sent in discreet, compliant packaging. These restriction lists are current as of March 2026 and may change — it is the buyer's ongoing responsibility to verify compliance.

Packaging, Handling, and Storage

All products are shipped in sealed, tamper-evident packaging appropriate for botanical raw materials. Products requiring temperature-controlled handling will be shipped with appropriate insulation and cold packs during warm months where feasible. Certificates of Analysis and batch documentation are included with each shipment.

Heat and Degradation

GACP LLC is not responsible for degradation of botanical compounds resulting from shipping conditions outside our control, including extreme temperatures during transit, extended holding at carrier facilities, or improper storage after delivery. Certain products (particularly oils and temperature-sensitive extracts) should be stored according to the product specification sheet upon receipt. If you are located in a region with extreme temperatures, consider selecting expedited shipping to minimize transit time.

Order Tracking

Tracking information is provided via email once your order ships. You can also check order status through your portal dashboard (coming soon).

Returns Policy

Due to the nature of botanical raw materials and the requirement to maintain chain-of-custody integrity, returns are accepted only under the following conditions:

Eligible for Return

Products that are damaged during shipping (reported within 48 hours of delivery with photographic evidence), products that do not match the stated specifications or Certificate of Analysis (verified by independent testing at buyer's expense), and incorrect products shipped in error by GACP LLC.

Not Eligible for Return

Products that have been opened, used, or had their tamper-evident seals broken; products returned due to buyer's change of mind, ordering error, or failure to verify jurisdictional legality prior to ordering; products returned after 14 calendar days from confirmed delivery; and products that have been stored improperly after delivery.

Return Process

Contact us through the contact page with your order number and detailed reason for return. Do not ship products back without prior authorization. GACP LLC will provide return shipping instructions and a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. Returns received without an RMA number will not be processed and will be returned to the sender at their expense.

Refunds

Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days of receiving and inspecting the returned product. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable except in cases of shipping damage or GACP LLC error. For orders placed at volume discount pricing, refunds may be adjusted to reflect the applicable tier pricing after the return.

Damaged or Missing Shipments

If your shipment arrives damaged, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photographs of both the external packaging and the damaged product. If your shipment is missing or lost in transit, contact us and we will file a claim with the carrier. Replacement or refund will be issued upon resolution of the carrier claim. GACP LLC is not responsible for packages confirmed as delivered by the carrier but claimed as not received — we recommend selecting shipping options with signature confirmation for valuable orders.

Contact

For shipping inquiries, return requests, or any order-related questions, please contact us through our contact page.
