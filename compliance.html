Our commitment to quality, traceability, and controlled access.

Our Commitment to Quality

GACP LLC is committed to supplying botanical ingredients and bioceutical compounds that meet the highest standards of quality, purity, and traceability. Our quality program is designed to support trade customers who operate within regulated frameworks, including dietary supplement manufacturing, food production, and research applications.

Certificates of Analysis

Every product shipped by GACP is accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from an accredited third-party laboratory. Our CoAs document identity verification, potency analysis (where applicable), and contaminant testing including heavy metals, microbial load, pesticide residues, and solvent residues. CoAs are available for review in our CoA Library prior to purchase by registered trade accounts.

For full CoA methodology, retain-sample handling, and the quality dispute process, see the Quality Assurance & CoA Policy.

Quality Standards

GACP's quality program reflects the following principles:

  • Supplier vetting against quality standards that may include current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) expectations where relevant to the product category;
  • Third-party laboratory testing of every batch for identity, purity, and contaminant screening;
  • Traceability documentation from source through shipment;
  • Compliance with U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) import requirements for botanical raw materials, including FDA prior notice and entry documentation; and
  • Ongoing monitoring of regulatory developments affecting product availability and classification.

Product Classification

Products sold by GACP are classified as raw material ingredients intended for use by qualified manufacturers, formulators, and researchers. GACP does not market or sell finished consumer dietary supplements directly to end consumers through gacp.llc. Product descriptions, specifications, and informational content on this website are directed to trade professionals.

Controlled Access

GACP operates a tiered access model. Certain products are available only to verified trade accounts that have completed GACP's account verification process. Access to restricted product listings may require confirmation of business registration, intended use, and jurisdictional compliance. Product availability is controlled dynamically by product and jurisdiction; some materials may be unavailable in certain states or territories.

For more information, see the Restricted Products & Account Verification Policy.

Safety Reporting

GACP takes product safety seriously. If you become aware of any safety concern potentially associated with a GACP product, please report it to info@gacp.llc. GACP will evaluate all reports, investigate as appropriate, document findings, and where GACP has a legal reporting obligation under applicable law, will report accordingly.
