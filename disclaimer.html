Drug Interaction Warning

Botanical alkaloids and bioactive compounds may interact with medications affecting the central nervous system. In particular, products containing kratom alkaloids (mitragynine, 7-hydroxymitragynine) interact with opioid receptors and should not be combined with opioid medications, benzodiazepines, sedatives, or alcohol. Products containing kanna alkaloids (mesembrine) exhibit serotonergic activity and should not be combined with SSRIs (selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors), SNRIs, MAO inhibitors, or other serotonergic medications due to the risk of serotonin syndrome. Hemp-derived cannabinoid products may interact with medications metabolized by cytochrome P450 enzymes. Always consult your healthcare provider about potential interactions, including enzyme inhibition specifically related to CYP enzymes, before using any botanical product.
