Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Our Commitment

GACP LLC is committed to ensuring that gacp.llc is accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).

Measures We Take

We endeavor to ensure accessibility by:

  • Using semantic HTML and ARIA attributes to support assistive technologies;
  • Maintaining sufficient color contrast ratios throughout the site;
  • Providing text alternatives for non-text content;
  • Ensuring keyboard navigability for all interactive elements; and
  • Designing responsive layouts that function across devices and screen sizes.

Known Limitations

We are aware that some areas of the site may not yet meet all WCAG 2.1 AA criteria. We are actively working to identify and resolve accessibility issues as part of our ongoing site development.

Feedback

If you encounter any accessibility barriers on gacp.llc, please contact us at info@gacp.llc. We welcome your feedback and will make reasonable efforts to address reported issues promptly.
